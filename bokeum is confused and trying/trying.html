    20240126 Newsfactory Friday Gourmet Dinner Kimjinhong's Beef Rib Soup Recipe

  1. Clean Korean beef rib(1kg) with a paper towel.
  2. In boiling water (3L) add the ribs and 30 black peppercorns (10 per 200g) and boil on medium heat for one hour.
  3. Take the meat out and cook with roasted green onion (remove burnt parts) + 1L of water + 7tbsp light soy sauce and reduce liquid on medium heat for 30 minutes.
  4. Put the seasoned meat back into the original broth and boil on low heat for 20 minutes.
